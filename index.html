Hi my name is Maddy Bettencourt. I am a graphic design student at the University of Massachusetts Dartmouth. I fell in love with graphic design in high school when I chose it as my vocational shop. In my free time I like to work on improving my skills in design and illustration, listen to music, or watch movies. I place a lot of value on hard work and integrity.
